# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its Code of Conduct. By participating in this project — as a contributor, reviewer, issue reporter, or otherwise — you agree to abide by its terms.

The full text is canonical and lives at the link above. A short summary:

- Treat everyone with respect. Assume good intent.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disagreement is fine; hostility is not.
- The maintainers may take action — including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans — against participants whose conduct violates this policy.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to `spook@spookylabs.ai`. Reports will be reviewed and responded to within a reasonable time. Reporters' identities will be kept confidential.

The maintainers are obligated to respect reporter confidentiality and to enforce this policy in good faith.
